Fruition Brewing is a new brewery and kitchen coming soon to the East Lake Village in Watsonville. We are focused on producing a wide variety of beers for our tasting room and patio, with a dedication to local and sustainable ingredients. And...

We're Hiring!

Fruition Brewing is looking for talented and passionate people to join our small team. Ideal candidates are dependable, honest, hardworking, dedicated, and trustworthy with a good attitude. We are looking for people with customer service skills, who enjoy engaging with customers and are enthusiastic about beer and fermentation. Applicants should consider themselves a team player and take pride in communicating effectively with customers and fellow employees. Because we are a production facility and tasting room, we expect all employees to put safety first and adhere to all protocols and procedures.

Job Requirements

  • Love of craft beer
  • Commitment to quality
  • Experience in beverage and/or restaurant service
  • A personal commitment to cleanliness and organization
  • ServSafe Certification
  • LEAD Certification (or ability to get one within 60 days)
  • Ability to lift 60+ lbs
  • Ability to work on your feet for long periods
  • Ability to pass a post-offer, pre-employment background check
  • Must be 21+ years of age

Starting pay $13/hr + tips

To apply, please submit a cover letter explaining why you want this job and what you would bring to the Fruition team, along with your resume and availability to info@fruitionbrewing.com with the subject line “Employment at Fruition”.
